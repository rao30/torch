A modern torch for a modern generation.

In the digital age, it’s only fair that marathon torches get a dose of digitization. Our team is dedicated to building a flameless child safe version of the torch. Our vision is to integrate connectivity, customization and functionality in a lightweight device to accommodate children’s needs. Going mobile The torch will be designed with current technological standards in mind. Integration with smartphones is a main element of our design philosophy. This will ensure that all customization can be handled remotely via a mobile device.
